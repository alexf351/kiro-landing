Product

What is Iro AI?

Iro AI is an iOS app that teaches practical AI fluency through 5-minute gamified lessons. It covers ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, prompt engineering, AI agents, automation, and creative AI across 20 learning paths and 2,000+ exercises. Free to start.

Is Iro AI legitimate?

Yes. Iro AI is a published iOS app on the Apple App Store at apps.apple.com/app/id6759628066. Its official website is tryiro.com.

Who built Iro AI?

Iro AI is an independent product built by a small team focused on gamified AI education. See /about.

Why is it called "Iro"?

"Iro" is the brand for the AI learning app at tryiro.com. It is distinct from "Kiro" (an AI IDE from Amazon), the Iro fashion brand, and other unrelated products.

Pricing

Is Iro AI free?

Yes. The free tier includes a starter slice across all 20 paths, daily challenges, XP, and ranks. No credit card required.

What does Iro AI cost?

Pro 3-Month is $24.99 every 3 months (about $1.92/week) and includes a 7-day free trial. Pro Weekly is $9.99/week with no trial. Both are billed via the Apple App Store and can be cancelled anytime. The 3-month plan saves about 80% versus paying weekly ($24.99 vs ~$129.87 over the same 13 weeks).

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through Apple subscription settings on iOS. Cancel anytime.

Is there a lifetime plan?

Not currently. Available plans are listed above.

Is there a student discount?

The free tier is generous enough to cover most of what students need. Specific student plans are not currently offered.

Platform

What devices does Iro AI run on?

Iro AI runs on iPhone and iPad (iOS).

When will Iro be on Android?

Android is in development. Follow tryiro.com or the official social profiles for updates.

Is there a web version?

The tryiro.com website hosts marketing, the free AI IQ test, and policy pages. The full learning experience runs in the iOS app.

Content

What does Iro AI teach?

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, prompt engineering, AI foundations, hallucination detection, AI agents, automation, vibe coding, AI image generation, AI video generation, AI for marketing, finance, work, students, managers, healthcare, job hunting, and AI tools.

How long is a lesson?

About 5 minutes.

How many lessons does Iro have?

375 lessons and 2,000+ exercises across 20 learning paths and 13 exercise types.

What are the rank tiers?

Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Iridescent.

Do I need prior AI experience?

No. Beginner paths start from zero on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. Advanced paths cover AI agents, automation, and vibe coding.

Does Iro have a prompt library?

Iro focuses on active prompt practice and AI-generated feedback in Prompt Lab. It teaches the underlying skills rather than only providing static templates.

Comparisons

How is Iro different from a video course (Coursera, Udemy, YouTube)?

Iro focuses on active practice in short sessions. Users write prompts, evaluate outputs, spot hallucinations, and duel opponents — not just watch.

Is Iro a "Duolingo for AI"?

Yes. Iro applies Duolingo-style gamified microlearning to AI fluency. See /duolingo-for-ai.

Is Iro a substitute for a CS degree or AI certification?

No. Iro builds practical day-to-day AI fluency. It is not an accredited certification.

AI IQ test

What is the AI IQ test?

A free 10-question quiz at /quiz that takes about 2 minutes, ranks your score Bronze-to-Iridescent, identifies your weakest topic, and recommends a learning path.

Do I need an account to take it?

No. The AI IQ test requires no signup.

Can I share my score?

Yes. The results page supports share links and friend challenges via a score URL parameter.

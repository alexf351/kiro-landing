## Automation is more than connecting tools

AI automation works best when the underlying workflow is clear. Learners need to know what should happen, what can go wrong, what data is needed, and where humans should review outputs.
## What Iro teaches

- The difference between prompts, workflows, automations, and agents.
- How to describe tasks in a way AI systems can follow.
- Where to add verification steps.
- How automation fits with prompt engineering and AI foundations.

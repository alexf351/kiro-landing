# An AI course alternative built for daily practice.

> Iro is for learners who want practical AI fluency but do not want to start with a long course. The app turns AI learning into short, guided, gamified practice.

## Why learners look for alternatives

Long AI courses can be hard to finish, especially when tools change quickly. A daily-practice app can help users build habits while staying closer to current tools and workflows.
## How Iro fits

- Beginner-friendly AI foundations.
- Tool paths for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and more.
- Prompt Lab and hands-on exercises.
- Duels, daily challenges, XP, streaks, and ranks.

## FAQ

**Is Iro a bootcamp?**  
No. Iro is a mobile AI learning app focused on short active lessons.

**Can I use Iro alongside a course?**  
Yes. Iro can reinforce AI skills through daily practice while other resources provide deep dives.

**Is Iro free?**  
Iro is free to start, with optional Pro upgrades through the iOS App Store.

## Why finance needs deliberate AI practice

Finance work is high-context and high-stakes. Casual AI use can be helpful for summarizing a 10-K or drafting a memo, but the same tools can also invent figures, misread a footnote, or quietly drop an assumption. Iro is designed to build the judgment loop: ask the right question, supply the right context, evaluate the answer, and own the result.

The finance path teaches AI as leverage on top of fundamentals you already have. It is not a replacement for CFA, CPA, MBA, or other accredited programs, and it is not financial advice.
## What you practice in Iro

- Prompting AI for company research, comparable analysis, and industry primers.
- Summarizing filings, transcripts, and long PDFs with structure preserved.
- Drafting and reviewing Excel/Sheets formulas and Python snippets with AI.
- Catching hallucinations in numbers, dates, citations, and accounting language.
- Writing investor updates, client memos, and one-pagers using AI as a co-editor.
## Who this is for

The finance path is built for analysts, associates, founders, finance-curious students, and operators who handle FP&A, fundraising, or reporting. It pairs well with the AI for work, AI agents, and prompt engineering paths.

## FAQ

**Can AI replace a financial analyst?**  
No. Today's AI tools can speed up research, drafting, summarization, and code-assisted analysis, but they still require human judgment for accuracy, context, and accountability. Iro teaches how to use AI as leverage, not as a replacement.

**Is Iro a finance certification?**  
No. Iro is a gamified mobile app that teaches practical AI skills. It is not an accredited finance certification (CFA, CPA, etc.) and is not financial advice.

**Which AI tools does the finance path cover?**  
Iro teaches transferable skills that apply to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity, plus general AI workflow patterns useful in modeling, research, and reporting.

**Will Iro give me financial advice?**  
No. Iro teaches how to use AI tools effectively. It does not provide personalized financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.

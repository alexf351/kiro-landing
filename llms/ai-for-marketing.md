## Why marketers need AI fluency

AI can accelerate marketing work, but vague prompts often create generic copy. The marketing skill is knowing what context matters: audience, offer, differentiation, channel, tone, and success criteria.
## Marketing skills Iro reinforces

- Prompting for audience-specific content.

- Turning research into messaging and briefs.
- Using AI as a collaborator while keeping human judgment.

## Why students should learn AI skills

AI tools are becoming part of school, internships, and early-career work. Students need more than shortcuts. They need to know how to ask, verify, revise, and use AI ethically and effectively.
## What Iro teaches students

- Prompting for explanations, examples, and study plans.
- Checking AI answers against sources and course requirements.
- Using AI for brainstorming without losing original thinking.
- Understanding major tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity.

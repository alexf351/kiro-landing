## Why AI at work needs training

AI adoption at work fails when teams treat AI like a magic search box. Useful results require clear goals, context, constraints, review, and an understanding of privacy and verification.
## Work skills Iro covers

- Prompting for professional outputs and structured drafts.
- Reviewing AI-generated analysis and summaries.
- Understanding agents, automation, and workflow design.
- Building daily practice instead of one-off experimentation.

## Why job hunting needs deliberate AI practice

The bar for an AI-written application is now invisible. Recruiters can spot generic GPT prose, and ATS systems penalize keyword stuffing. Lazy AI use makes you blend in; thoughtful AI use makes you stand out.

Iro teaches the difference. You'll learn how to brief AI on your actual experience, generate tailored drafts, edit them to sound like you, and prep for interviews with realistic role-play.
## What you practice in Iro

- Briefing AI with your real experience, achievements, and metrics.
- Tailoring résumés and cover letters per role without losing voice.
- Cold outreach to recruiters and hiring managers that does not read like spam.
- Mock interview prep — behavioral, technical, and case formats.
- Researching companies, teams, and interviewers efficiently.
## Who this is for

The job-hunting path is built for students entering the workforce, professionals making a move, career switchers, and anyone applying at scale. It pairs well with prompt engineering and AI for work.

## FAQ

**Will recruiters know I used AI?**  
If you copy-paste, yes. Iro teaches how to use AI as a drafting and review partner so your final application reads as your own.

**Can I use Iro to write my whole résumé?**  
You can draft with AI, but the most effective résumés are edited by a human (you) who knows the real story behind the bullets. Iro builds that editing skill.

**Does Iro work for technical interviews?**  
Yes. The path covers behavioral, case, and technical interview practice across AI tools.

**Which AI tools does the job-hunting path cover?**  
Skills transfer across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity.

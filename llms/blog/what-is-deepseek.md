## What is DeepSeek?

**DeepSeek is an AI company, based in China, known for building powerful AI models that cost far less than many rivals — and for releasing several of them openly.** Its models, including well-regarded reasoning and coding models, made headlines for matching pricier competitors at a fraction of the cost. You can use DeepSeek free through its app and website, much like ChatGPT, and developers can run several of its open models themselves.

## What is DeepSeek good at?

- **Reasoning** — its reasoning models work through multi-step problems well.
- **Coding** — a frequent strength in its model line.
- **Cost efficiency** — strong performance at low or no cost, which is its headline appeal.
- **Openness** — several models are openly available for developers to run and build on.

For most people, using DeepSeek looks just like any other AI assistant: open the app or site, type a request in plain language, and refine the answer. ## What to consider before using DeepSeek

One practical note: DeepSeek is built by a China-based company, and some employers, schools, and governments restrict tools based on where data is processed. **Before using it for sensitive or work data, check your organization's policy** — the same caution applies to any AI tool. For general learning and personal use, it's a capable, low-cost option. Weighing trade-offs like this is part of [AI literacy](/blog/what-is-ai-literacy).

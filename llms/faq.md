# Iro AI — Frequently Asked Questions

> Comprehensive FAQ about Iro AI: pricing, platform, content, comparisons, privacy, support, and more.

**Canonical FAQ page:** https://tryiro.com/faq
**Last updated:** 2026-07-23

## About Iro AI

### What is Iro AI?

Iro AI is a gamified app that turns learning AI into a daily game: pick a path, complete 5-minute lessons, practice real prompts, earn XP and streaks, and build a visible skill profile (your Operator Build). It covers ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, prompt engineering, AI agents, automation, and creative AI across 20 learning paths and 2,000+ exercises. iOS-first, also in the browser at https://app.tryiro.com. Free to start with a hearts system.

### Is Iro AI legitimate?

Yes. Iro AI is a published iOS app on the Apple App Store at https://apps.apple.com/app/id6759628066. Its official website is https://tryiro.com.

### Who built Iro AI?

Iro AI is an independent product built by a small team focused on gamified AI education. See https://tryiro.com/about.

### Why is it called "Iro"?

"Iro" is the brand for the AI learning app at https://tryiro.com. It is distinct from "Kiro" (an AI IDE from Amazon), the Iro fashion brand, and other unrelated products.

## Pricing

### Is Iro AI free?

Yes. You can use Iro for free with a hearts system, similar to Duolingo. Hearts let you keep practicing and refill over time. Iro Pro removes the limits and unlocks the full learning experience: unlimited practice, custom AI learning paths, Ask Iro guidance, progress tracking, and all 16 exercise types. You can start with a 7-day free trial and upgrade only if you want the unlimited experience.

### What does Iro AI cost?

Pro Annual is $59.99 a year (about $1.15/week) and includes a 7-day free trial. Pro Weekly is $9.99/week with no trial. Both are billed via the Apple App Store. The annual plan saves about 88% versus weekly ($59.99 vs ~$519.48 over a year). Cancel anytime.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through Apple subscription settings on iOS. Cancel anytime.

### Is there a lifetime plan?

Not currently. Available plans are listed above.

## Platform

### What devices does Iro AI run on?

Iro AI runs on iPhone and iPad (iOS), and in any modern browser at https://app.tryiro.com with account sync between the two.

### When will Iro be on Android?

Android is in development. In the meantime, Android users can learn in the browser at https://app.tryiro.com — progress carries into the app when it launches.

### Is there a web app?

Yes. The full learning experience also runs in the browser at https://app.tryiro.com — same account, lessons, and progress as the iOS app. The tryiro.com site itself hosts the free AI rank quiz, guides, and policy pages.

## Content

### What does Iro AI teach?

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, prompt engineering, AI foundations, hallucination detection, AI agents, automation, vibe coding, AI image generation, AI video generation, AI for marketing, finance, work, students, managers, healthcare, job hunting, and AI tools.

### How long is a lesson?

About 5 minutes.

### How many lessons does Iro have?

375 lessons and 2,000+ exercises across 20 learning paths and 16 exercise types.

### What are the rank tiers?

Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Iridescent.

### What is the Operator Build?

Your visible AI skill profile. Iro scores your ability across areas like prompting, tools, automation, creative AI, and work skills, rolls them into an Operator Build with a power level, and tells you exactly what to train next. It lives in the Progress tab alongside stats, achievements, and certificates.

### How is the app organized?

Three tabs: Learn (your paths and 5-minute lessons on a Duolingo-style map), Train (Prompt Lab, prompt practice, and live AI duels), and Progress (your Operator Build, stats, achievements, and certificates).

### Do I need prior AI experience?

No. Beginner paths start from zero on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. Advanced paths cover AI agents, automation, and vibe coding.

### Does Iro have a prompt library?

Iro focuses on active prompt practice and AI-generated feedback in Prompt Lab. It teaches the underlying skills rather than only providing static templates.

## Comparisons

### How is Iro different from a video course (Coursera, Udemy, YouTube)?

Iro focuses on active practice in short sessions. Users write prompts, evaluate outputs, spot hallucinations, and duel opponents — not just watch.

### Is Iro a "Duolingo for AI"?

Yes. Iro applies Duolingo-style gamified microlearning to AI fluency. See https://tryiro.com/duolingo-for-ai.

### Is Iro a substitute for a CS degree or AI certification?

No. Iro builds practical day-to-day AI fluency. It is not an accredited certification.

## AI IQ test

### What is the AI IQ test?

A free 10-question quiz at https://tryiro.com/quiz that takes about 2 minutes, ranks your score on a Bronze-to-Diamond scale (Iridescent is unlocked in the app), identifies your weakest topic, and recommends a learning path.

### Do I need an account to take it?

No. The AI IQ test requires no signup.

### Can I share my score?

Yes. The results page supports share links and friend challenges via the `?score=` URL parameter.

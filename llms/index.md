## What Iro AI is

Iro AI is a mobile microlearning app for AI fluency. It is closer to Duolingo than to Coursera or Udemy — active practice, short daily reps, gamified progression, and competitive duels instead of long video lectures.

## What you can do in Iro

- **18 learning paths** across Tool Mastery, Creative & Coding, Work & Career, and Core Skills
- **345 lessons** and **2,075+ exercises** across 13 exercise types
- **Prompt Lab** — write real prompts and receive AI-generated feedback
- **Live duels** — 5-question ELO-ranked speed rounds against 60+ unique opponents
- **Weekly AI news briefings**
- **Daily challenges** and **streaks**
- **XP and 6 ranks** — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Iridescent

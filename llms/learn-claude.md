## Why Claude deserves its own learning path

Claude is often used for writing, analysis, planning, coding help, and longer-context tasks. The challenge is knowing how to structure context so the model can produce useful answers without inventing details or drifting away from the goal.

Iro teaches Claude as part of a broader AI fluency system: prompts, evaluation, hallucination awareness, and workflow design.

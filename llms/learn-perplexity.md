## Why AI research needs judgment

AI search can speed up research, but it does not remove the need to evaluate sources, compare claims, and understand uncertainty. Iro teaches research habits that help users get more value from tools like Perplexity.
## What you practice

- Writing specific research questions.
- Checking whether sources actually support the answer.
- Separating summaries from verified facts.
- Using AI tools for exploration, not blind trust.

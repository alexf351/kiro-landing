The Iro Path Library is a directory of every learning path in the Iro AI app. Each path teaches one AI tool, skill, or job use-case through short, hands-on lessons with instant feedback — the active-practice method that beats passive video courses. You can start any path free, and Iro can also generate a custom path on almost any topic you type in.

Questions people ask

What is the Iro Path Library?

It's a directory of every learning path inside the Iro AI app. Each path teaches one AI tool, skill, or job use-case through short, hands-on lessons with feedback. You can start any path free and upgrade to Pro for full access.

Do I have to pay to start a path?

No. Iro is free to start — you get starter lessons in a path of your choice, a sample of every exercise type, and a taste of Ask Iro, your AI coach. Pro unlocks every lesson and every path, plus unlimited custom paths, for $59.99 a year (about $1.15 a week) with a 7-day free trial, or $9.99 a week.

Can Iro teach a topic that isn't listed here?

Yes. Beyond these built-in paths, Iro's Custom Paths feature can generate a path on almost any topic you type in — from a specific AI workflow to a broader skill — and turn it into hands-on lessons.

How is this different from a video course?

Video is passive — you watch, then forget. Iro makes you write real prompts, compare model outputs, and spot mistakes, then gives feedback. Active practice is what actually builds skill, and each lesson takes about five minutes.

Is there an Android app?

Android is in active development. Iro is iOS-only for now, but you can join the Android waitlist on the home page, and in the meantime Iro runs in any browser at app.tryiro.com — your progress carries into the app when it lands.
